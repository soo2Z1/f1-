FORMULA I

is a world championship in open-wheeled circuit racing , which takes place under the auspices of the International Automobile Federation (FIA) . First of all , "formula" is a term that defines a set of rules that are mandatory for all participants in the race.

Formula 1 features 10 teams and 20 drivers, each competing at the highest level of motorsport.
